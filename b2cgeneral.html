B2C General Information

About the b2c site

bonusbox's b2c site is a payback system where users can earn points on purchases made through OCP and affiliate shops. Points can be redeemed on the b2c site for redemptions.

The b2c site allows users to browse OCP and affiliate shops. The homepage lists different groupings of shops: last visted/favorite shops, shops based on categories (clothing & accessories, food & drink, and electronics & computres) as well as by shops recommended by bonusbox.

Users can also view shops via the category dropdown in the navigation bar. There is also a search engine, our focus is not being placed here.

Redemptions are also viewable via the redemption page. Individual brands also have their own pages which show which redemptions they offer.

Users can view their point summary, which consits of the OCP or affiliate shop a purchse was made through, the date, and amounto f points earned. Overal points, redeemed points, and current point balance are also displayed.
