Redemptions: General Information

Overview

The bonusbox b2c site offers redemptions for points earned on purchases made through OCP and affiliate shops. The redemptions can be found on:
  • The redemptions page - This page informers the user of ways he/she can redeem his/her points for rewards (actual items or giftcards)
  • Brands pages - These are individual brand pages which show all redemptions offered by a specifc brand


Components of the Redemption Page

The bonusbox redemption page is made up of two sections:
  • Hero Brands
  • Redemption Tiles
Hero Brands:


Hero Brands are located at the top of the redemptions page. They consist of a brand mood image, logo, and three redemption suggestions. The three redemption suggestions come from 3 price points: souvenir, reward, aspirational.
Redemption Tiles:


no state hover state (enough points) hover state (not enough points)

Redemption tiles are located after the Brand Heros. Currently, the redemption tiles only consist of gift cards. Redemption tiles can be filtered by number of points (200P°-1000P°, 1001P°-2000P°,2001P°-5000P°, 5001P°-10000P°)
